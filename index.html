About Our Company

BALKAN RECYCLING DOO is a specialized provider of industrial and municipal solutions based in Novi Sad, Serbia. We focus on the sales and technical maintenance of professional equipment for:

  • Large-scale grass mowing and landscape management;
  • Wood chipping and forestry waste processing;
  • Waste recycling and environmental technologies.

We pride ourselves on our in-house service center, ensuring long-term reliability and professional warranty support for all machinery we supply. Our mission is to bring smart, autonomous, and eco-friendly technologies to the Balkan market.
